About me

I'm interested in cosmology, galaxy formation and evolution, and the first stars in the universe. I have been at the Georgia Institute of Technology since August 2017, in pursuit of my Phyiscs Ph.D.

I received my Bachelors of Science from the University of Washington in Seattle with a double major in Physics and Astronomy and a double minor in Mathematics and Philosophy.

I'm passionate about scientific outreach and accessibility, philosophy, art, and of course the brilliant universe we live in. After spending my days analyzing cosmological simulations, I get to come home to two adorable kitties I adopted at the Atlanta Humane Society.
